ABOUT US

SPASIUM is a platform that is designed to help you in finding exclusive artworks (art print) online for your interior, hotel, and commercial spaces. We offer licensed and ready-to-hang wall art from selected Indonesian talents in the simplest way, vary from photography, painting, graphic art, illustration, drawing, and more.

Read More...

Please Call Me if you have some problem in interior decoration and we will try to solve it.


All our artworks are delivered framed and securely packaged, ready-to-display for your convenience.


Browse our collection online from any device, we offer the simplest way in selecting and buying artworks.


Exclusivity is our concern. We offer individually selected artworks, printed in limited copies.
